Community Rules

Be respectful. Stay on topic. No financial advice. No spam or scams. English and Spanish are both welcome. Keep it legal, post in the correct channels, and do not harass people. Toxic behavior, repeated misuse of the structure, or sketchy direct messaging may result in removal.

Zero-Tolerance Support Rule

If support is supposed to happen through the community, tickets, or designated contacts, members must use those paths. Public-facing content, outside live streams, and unrelated channels are not the place to demand personal trading help, wallet help, or community support if the rules say otherwise.

If that structure is broken after clear notice, access may be removed immediately and without refund where permitted by law.

Confidentiality and Competing Communities
